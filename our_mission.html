Co-founders

From completely different backgrounds, Nikki and james met at the university of San Andreas, where Nikki was a Business Major and James studied Fashion. After they met during an event organized by the board of students, Nikki and James started to grow closer and closer. After a couple months, Nikki came up with this crazy idea of founding a company that encompasses both their skills into something unrealistic and new. After considering several different ideas, they settled on a fashion house for which Nikki would manage the business side and James would have a playground to make all his crazy ideas into material Chef d'oeuvres. which they managed to conduct efficiently. but after a couple years, selling locally wasnt enough of a challenge so they went after a more global customer base, thus the website. from a fashion house located in the Bronx, NY, Nikki's is now located in more than 50 countries around the world.
